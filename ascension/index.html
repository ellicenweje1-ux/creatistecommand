Privacy Policy

Ascension collects the details you submit on this page — your name, email address, phone number, Instagram username, occupation and how you heard about us — solely to review your application and, where you have opted in, to share updates from Ascension.

Your information is never sold or shared with third parties beyond the services we use to operate the guest list. You may request access to, or deletion of, your data at any time by contacting us.

Professional photography and videography take place at Ascension events; imagery may be used across Ascension's marketing channels. Concerns can be raised with a member of staff on arrival.
